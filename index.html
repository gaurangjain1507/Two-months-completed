Happy 2 Months, My Pookie!
Do mahine… sirf calendar ke liye kuch tareekhein hongi. Aur jab tu lips bite karti hai, I swear… mera pura silly-crazy, thoda horny, thoda romantic mood on ho jata hai. 😳❤️ Tu meri hoodie pehle hi le chuki hai… aur main us hoodie se teri khushboo chura kar sone lagta hoon. Main tujhe har din woh pyaar dunga, jisme har raat tu safe feel kare, aur har subah tu sirf itna kahe — "I belong here." Tu sirf meri pookie nahi hai, tu meri addiction hai — meri smile, mera peace, mera mess bhi… aur meri jaan bhi.
